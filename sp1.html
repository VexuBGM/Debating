IMPORTANCE

Why is the First speaker important

Sets the tone of the debate

The first speaker is responsible for presenting the team's opening argument and establishing the key themes that will be developed throughout the debate. As such, they play a critical role in setting the tone for the debate and framing the issues that will be discussed.

Builds momentum

A strong performance by the first speaker can create a sense of momentum that carries the team forward throughout the debate. By presenting a compelling and persuasive argument, the first speaker can inspire the rest of the team to build on their ideas and present an even stronger case.

Establishes credibility

The first speaker is often seen as the face of the team and is responsible for establishing the team's credibility with the audience. A strong, confident presentation by the first speaker can go a long way in establishing the team's authority on the topic being debated.

You must

Things you can do to make a good impression

Rebuttal

If you are opposition you need to rebbutal the proposition team. This should take 1 - 2 minutes. Talk about their arguments and why they are not right.

Definitions

The first speaker sets the definitions and how they understand the motion. As opposition you need to say whether you agree or disagree with the other team's definitions. If you disagree then you need to set your new definitions and the second speaker of the proposition needs to agree or disagree.

Road map

As the first speaker you need to say what your team will do and how the debate is going to go. Then you should make a road map of your speech.

Present your team's arguments and get into details for one of them

You should say with one or two words all your arguments without getting into details and you must choose one of them and get into details for it.

TEMPLATE

How your speech should look like

1st step

Start with greeting.
Introduce yourself and your team.
Introduce the topic and your position on it
Then continue with the rebbutal if you are opposition
Then say your 3 arguments name by name but don't go into details.
Make a road map of the debate and your speech

2nd step

You can start the 2nd step with "I will get into our motion". Use these phrases:

  • our house...
  • our arguments are 1st, 2nd, 3rd

Say: we belive this because... and defend it
you need to set definitions as proposition
as opposition you can agree or disagree with the definitions and set your own.

3rd step

Talk about one of your arguments in details.
