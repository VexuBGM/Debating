The rules of school debating

Click here to see all ther rules in school dabating

The most important rules:

  1. The first, second and third speaker has 8 minutes to talk and after that 15 seconds to conclude their spech.
  2. The reply speaker has 4 minutes to talk and during his/her spech the opposing team can't ask points of intrest (POIs).
  3. The third and reply speaker can't add new arguments.
  4. POIs can be asked after the first minute of the speach and you can't ask any POIs after the 7th minute.
  5. After somebody asks a POI they can't ask another for 45 seconds.
  6. You can bring your notes on the stage where you talk and your phone to look how much time you have left.
  7. You can't talk loud during anybody's speach but you can write notes to each other.
